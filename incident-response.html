Incident Response & Recovery

Rapid containment, investigation, and recovery to minimize business impact.

Overview

When a security incident occurs, every minute matters. Fast, structured response can dramatically reduce the impact of ransomware, account compromise, data loss, or system outages.

We help organizations prepare for, respond to, and recover from cybersecurity incidents with clear processes, expert guidance, and proven response playbooks.

What We Respond To

  • Ransomware and malware outbreaks
  • Compromised accounts and identity breaches
  • Phishing‑driven access or data exposure
  • Unauthorized access or privilege escalation
  • Data loss or data exfiltration events
  • System outages caused by security events

Incident Response Capabilities

  • Containment: Stop the spread and limit attacker movement.
  • Investigation: Identify root cause, scope, and impact.
  • Eradication: Remove malicious activity and restore integrity.
  • Recovery: Bring systems back online safely and securely.
  • Forensics: Preserve evidence and analyze attacker behavior.
  • Post‑Incident Review: Strengthen controls to prevent recurrence.

Typical Engagements

  • Emergency response to active security incidents
  • Ransomware containment and recovery
  • Compromised account investigation and remediation
  • Forensic analysis and reporting
  • Development of incident response plans and playbooks
  • Tabletop exercises and readiness assessments

Our Approach

  1. Identify: Confirm the incident and assess severity.
  2. Contain: Limit damage and isolate affected systems.
  3. Investigate: Determine what happened and how.
  4. Eradicate: Remove malicious artifacts and restore integrity.
  5. Recover: Safely restore operations and validate systems.
  6. Improve: Document lessons learned and strengthen defenses.

Benefits

  • Reduced downtime and business disruption
  • Faster containment of active threats
  • Clear understanding of root cause and impact
  • Improved readiness for future incidents
  • Stronger long‑term security posture
