Email Security & Zero Trust Access

Protect identities, email, and access with modern Zero Trust security controls.

Overview

Email remains the #1 attack vector for phishing, credential theft, and business compromise. Zero Trust access ensures that only the right users, on the right devices, under the right conditions can access your systems.

We help organizations secure email, identity, and access using advanced filtering, authentication, and conditional access controls that dramatically reduce risk.

Email Security Capabilities

  • Advanced Threat Protection: Block phishing, malware, and impersonation attacks.
  • Spam & Policy Filtering: Reduce unwanted mail and enforce communication rules.
  • DMARC, DKIM & SPF: Protect your domain from spoofing and fraud.
  • Safe Links & Safe Attachments: Real‑time scanning of URLs and files.
  • Quarantine & Reporting: Visibility into threats and user‑reported phishing.

Zero Trust Access Controls

  • Multi‑Factor Authentication (MFA): Strong identity verification.
  • Conditional Access: Enforce access rules based on risk, device, or location.
  • Passwordless Authentication: FIDO2 keys and Authenticator app.
  • Risk‑Based Access: Automated blocking of suspicious sign‑ins.
  • Session Controls: Limit access to sensitive data in risky scenarios.

Typical Engagements

  • Deployment of advanced email threat protection
  • Implementation of MFA and Conditional Access
  • Configuration of DMARC, DKIM, and SPF
  • Rollout of passwordless authentication
  • Zero Trust access design and enforcement

Our Approach

  1. Assess: Review current email security and access controls.
  2. Design: Build a Zero Trust access model tailored to your environment.
  3. Implement: Deploy filtering, authentication, and access policies.
  4. Integrate: Align identity, device, and email security.
  5. Optimize: Tune policies and improve user experience.

Benefits

  • Reduced phishing and credential theft
  • Stronger identity and access protection
  • Better control over who can access what
  • Improved compliance and audit readiness
  • Modern, secure authentication experience
