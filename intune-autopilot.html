Automation & Endpoint Management (Intune / Autopilot)

Modern, cloud‑based device provisioning, configuration, and management for your workforce.

Overview

Managing devices manually doesn’t scale. With Microsoft Intune and Windows Autopilot, you can provision, configure, and secure endpoints automatically — from anywhere. We design and operate modern endpoint management solutions that reduce effort, improve security, and deliver a better user experience.

From zero‑touch deployment to ongoing policy management, we help you move from traditional imaging to cloud‑based, automated endpoint management.

What We Deliver

  • Intune tenant configuration and best practices
  • Windows Autopilot deployment profiles
  • Device compliance and configuration policies
  • Application deployment and updates
  • Security baselines and hardening
  • Mobile device management (iOS/Android)
  • Reporting and monitoring for endpoints

Modern Endpoint Capabilities

  • Zero‑Touch Provisioning: Devices ship directly to users, ready to enroll.
  • Policy‑Driven Configuration: Standard builds enforced via Intune policies.
  • Unified Endpoint Management: Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android in one place.
  • Security Controls: Encryption, AV, firewall, and compliance rules.
  • App Lifecycle Management: Install, update, and retire apps centrally.
  • Remote Support: Manage and remediate devices from anywhere.

Typical Engagements

  • Design and rollout of Intune for endpoint management
  • Implementation of Windows Autopilot for new device deployment
  • Migration from traditional imaging (SCCM/GPO) to cloud‑based management
  • Creation of standard device profiles and policies
  • Integration with Azure AD and modern identity
  • Ongoing management and optimization of endpoint environment

Our Approach

  1. Assessment: Review current device management tools and processes.
  2. Design: Define Intune/Autopilot architecture, policies, and profiles.
  3. Pilot: Test with a subset of users and devices.
  4. Rollout: Expand to full fleet with structured deployment waves.
  5. Operate: Monitor, update, and refine policies and configurations.

Benefits

  • Faster onboarding for new employees
  • Consistent, secure device configurations
  • Reduced IT effort for imaging and setup
  • Better visibility into device health and compliance
  • Improved user experience with modern tools
