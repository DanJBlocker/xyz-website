Backup & Disaster Recovery

Protect your data. Ensure business continuity. Recover from anything.

Overview

Data loss is one of the most costly events an organization can face — whether caused by hardware failure, ransomware, human error, or natural disaster. Our Backup & Disaster Recovery (BDR) service ensures your business can withstand disruptions and recover quickly with minimal downtime.

We combine secure backups, automated testing, and a clear recovery strategy to keep your organization resilient and operational.

What We Protect

  • Servers and virtual machines
  • Workstations and critical endpoints
  • Microsoft 365 and cloud data
  • Databases and line‑of‑business applications
  • File shares and structured/unstructured data
  • Network configurations and system images

Backup Strategy

We design a multi‑layered backup strategy tailored to your environment:

  • Local backups: Fast recovery for day‑to‑day issues
  • Cloud backups: Off‑site protection for disaster scenarios
  • Immutable backups: Ransomware‑proof data copies
  • Versioning: Restore files from any point in time
  • Automated testing: Regular verification of backup integrity

Disaster Recovery Planning

We build a complete DR plan that ensures your business can recover quickly:

  • Recovery Time Objective (RTO) definition
  • Recovery Point Objective (RPO) definition
  • Failover and failback procedures
  • Communication and escalation plans
  • Cloud‑based DR environments (optional)
  • Annual DR testing and validation

Benefits

  • Protection against ransomware and cyberattacks
  • Reduced downtime and financial loss
  • Confidence that your data is always recoverable
  • Compliance with industry and regulatory requirements
  • Predictable, fully managed backup operations

How It Works

  1. Assessment: We evaluate your systems, data, and risk profile.
  2. Design: We build a backup and DR strategy aligned with your business needs.
  3. Deployment: Backups, agents, and cloud storage are configured.
  4. Monitoring: Backups are monitored 24/7 for success and integrity.
  5. Testing: Regular restore tests ensure everything works when needed.

Be Prepared for Anything

Don’t wait for a disaster to discover gaps in your backup strategy. Let’s build a resilient, secure recovery plan that protects your business.
