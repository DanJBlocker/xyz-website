Security Operations & Monitoring

Continuous visibility, detection, and response to keep your environment secure.

Overview

Security operations is about more than tools — it’s about having the right visibility, processes, and response capabilities in place. We help you build and operate a modern security operations function that monitors your environment, detects threats, and responds quickly.

Whether you need a “SOC‑lite” capability or a more advanced monitoring setup, we design security operations that fit your size, risk profile, and technology stack.

What We Monitor

  • Identities and access activity
  • Endpoints and servers
  • Network traffic and VPN activity
  • Cloud services and SaaS platforms
  • Email and collaboration tools
  • Security alerts from existing tools

Security Operations Capabilities

  • Log Collection & Visibility: Centralized logging across key systems.
  • Alerting & Triage: Prioritized alerts with clear playbooks.
  • Threat Detection: Use of rules, analytics, and threat intelligence.
  • Incident Handling: Defined workflows for investigation and response.
  • Reporting & Metrics: Security posture and trend reporting.
  • Continuous Improvement: Tuning rules and processes over time.

Typical Engagements

  • Design and implementation of a SOC‑lite capability
  • Deployment of logging and monitoring tools
  • Creation of alert rules and response playbooks
  • Integration of existing security tools into a unified view
  • Ongoing monitoring and incident support

Our Approach

  1. Assess: Review current visibility, tools, and incident processes.
  2. Design: Define monitoring scope, data sources, and workflows.
  3. Implement: Configure logging, alerts, and dashboards.
  4. Operate: Monitor, triage, and support incident handling.
  5. Optimize: Refine rules, coverage, and reporting over time.

Benefits

  • Improved visibility into security events
  • Faster detection of suspicious activity
  • More consistent incident response
  • Better use of existing security tools
  • Clearer understanding of your security posture
